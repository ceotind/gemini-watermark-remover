# Gemini Watermark Remover

A high-performance, browser-based utility designed to remove AI-generated watermarks from images created with Gemini AI. This tool prioritizes privacy and quality, ensuring your images remain on your device while being processed at full resolution.

## Core Capabilities

*   **Local Processing:** Images are handled directly within your browser. Your data never leaves your device, providing a secure environment for your content.
*   **Batch Operations:** Clean up to 10 images simultaneously to streamline your workflow.
*   **Quality Retention:** The underlying engine targets only the watermark area, preserving the original resolution, color accuracy, and details of your AI-generated art.
*   **Zero Cost:** Access the full feature set without subscriptions, credits, or account registration.
*   **Instant Results:** Process and download your images in high-quality PNG format in seconds.

## How to Use

1.  **Upload:** Drag and drop your Gemini-generated images into the workspace or click to browse your files.
2.  **Process:** The AI-powered engine identifies and removes the watermark automatically.
3.  **Download:** Save your watermark-free images individually or as a complete ZIP archive for batch uploads.

## Technical Standards

*   **Framework:** Built with Next.js for a responsive and stable user experience.
*   **Security:** Uses client-side execution to eliminate server-side storage risks.
*   **Compatibility:** Optimized for all modern web browsers.
